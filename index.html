What is typography?

You've probably seen a website that looked visually appeasing before, and you've probably seen a website that looks straight up horrendous. Well, what if I told you typography—the art of arranging letters and text in a way that makes the copy legible, clear, and visually appealing to the reader—is a major factor in making not only websites, but everything from newspapers to brand logos look the way they look? In this blog, we will discuss how typography works, the history of it, and how it is used today.
