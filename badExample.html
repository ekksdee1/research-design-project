Example 1

Taking one look at this website, you can see the creators did not practice good typography; the text is all over the place, isn't readable in some places, etc. The website even claims to be the world's worst website! Honestly, it feels like you could contract a disease from just looking at the website.

The large title on the front of the page is in a blocky and odd font, making it hard on the eyes. Everything on the website is cluttered, not organized, and the color combinations aren't the best either.
