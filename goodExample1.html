Example 2

Google Maps is a popular mapping platform offered by Google, launched in 2005. This is not a traditonal article or website, so you might say that the text is not organized, but considering the function of the website, the text is quite readable.

All of the text on the actual map is surrounded by a black outline, increasing the visibility of the text. The font is sans-serif, very simple. The interface itself is easy to understand and use. (You may have to click on "View larger map" to see the entire UI.)
